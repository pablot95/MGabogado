Services

Specialized legal advice on the Argentine pension system. We protect your rights and maximize your benefits with experience and dedication.

Pension Planning

Don't wait until retirement age to review your records. We analyze your work history years in advance to detect missing contributions and correct errors on time. We design a strategy today so your future retirement benefit is the maximum possible.

  • Work history analysis
  • Detection of missing contributions
  • Error correction
  • Retirement strategy
  • Benefit maximization

Retirement Benefits Management

Have you reached retirement age but are missing years of contributions? We analyze your work history and apply current moratorium programs so you can access your benefit. We handle the case filing and follow-up until payment.

  • Use of current moratorium programs
  • Case filing
  • Complete follow-up
  • Procedures before ANSES
  • Management until payment

Survivor Pensions

In difficult times, we handle the urgent paperwork. We manage direct or derived pensions for spouses, domestic partners, and children. We ensure the benefit is granted quickly to guarantee family support.

  • Direct pensions
  • Derived pensions
  • For spouses and domestic partners
  • For minor children
  • Expedited processing

Benefit Readjustment

Do you feel your retirement benefit doesn't reflect what you contributed during your working years? We analyze whether ANSES miscalculated your initial benefit or if you're entitled to an update based on court rulings (Badaro, Elliff cases, etc.). We claim the difference.

  • Initial calculation analysis
  • Benefit mobility claims
  • Benefit updates
  • Difference claims
  • Back payments

Disability Retirement

If a health condition prevents you from continuing to work before retirement age, we advise you before the Medical Commissions to obtain temporary or permanent disability retirement.

  • Contribution regularity calculation
  • Temporary retirement
  • Permanent retirement
  • Procedures before Medical Commissions
  • Appeals and reviews

Healthcare Injunctions

Are you retiring and being forced to switch to PAMI? We file injunction actions so you can keep your original Health Insurance or Private Healthcare plan without paying excessive extra costs. We protect your medical coverage.

  • Injunction actions
  • Health Insurance preservation
  • Private Healthcare maintenance
  • Avoid excessive costs
  • Medical coverage protection

Other Services

We offer specialized advice on particular cases within the Argentine pension system, including service recognition, teacher retirement, special regimes, and international agreements.

  • Service Recognition
  • Teacher Retirement
  • Differential Regimes
  • Hazardous Work
  • International Agreement Retirement
